---
title: Chicken Ricotta Meatballs with Spinach Alfredo Sauce
categories: 
  - Meals
date: 2026-01-12
featured_image: https://luna-za-tuna.github.io/Personal_Recipe/images/
recipe:
  servings: 4-6
  prep: n/a
  cook: n/a
  ingredients_markdown: |-
    * 1 lb ground chicken
    * 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
    * 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    * 1 egg
    * 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
    * 2 cloves garlic, minced
    * 1 tbsp Italian seasoning
    * Salt and pepper, to taste For the Sauce:
    * 2 tbsp butter
    * 1 clove garlic, minced
    * 1 cup heavy cream
    * 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    * 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped Salt and pepper, to taste 
  directions_markdown: |-
    1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
    2. In a large bowl, combine the ground chicken, ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan, egg, breadcrumbs, minced garlic, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
    3. Form the mixture into 1-inch meatballs and place them on the prepared baking sheet.
    4. Bake the meatballs for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown and fully cooked.
    5. While the meatballs are baking, prepare the sauce. Melt the butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
    6. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute until fragrant.
    7. Pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a simmer.
    8. stir in the grated Parmesan cheese and cook until the sauce thickens, about 3-5 minutes.
    9. Add the chopped spinach and cook until wilted, about 2 minutes. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
    10. Once the meatballs are done, add them to the skillet with the sauce and gently toss to coat.
    11. Serve the meatballs and spinach Alfredo sauce over pasta, rice, or as desired. Garnish with additional Parmesan cheese if preferred.
