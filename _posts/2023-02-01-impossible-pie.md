    * 1/2 cup flour

  directions_markdown: |-

    2. Add all ingredients to blender and blend till mixed thoroughly 
    3. Pour mixture into greased pie pan
    4. Bake in oven for 1 hour
    5. Let cool and then store in put in fridge till chilled
