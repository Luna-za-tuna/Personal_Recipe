  ingredients_markdown: |-
    * 1 lb chicken breast
    * 1/2 tsp each of salt and pepper
    * 1 tsp each of paprika and onion powder
    * 1 TBSP minced garlic
    * 1/2 - 1 cup Buffalo Wild Wings Parmesan Garlic sauce
    * 1 jar of Alfredo sauce
    * 12 oz spaghetti
    * 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
    * 6 slices of bacon cooked and chopped 
  directions_markdown: |-
    1. Add the chicken, salt, pepper, paprika, and onion powder to a slow cooker. 
    2. Add minced garlic and enough of the garlic parm sauce to fully cover the chicken (I do not use the entire bottle). 
    3. Add one jar of Alfredo sauce. Cook on low for 4-6 hours.
    4. Shred or chop up chicken and add the shredded cheddar cheese. 
    5. Boil spaghetti according to the directions on the package.
    6. Add cooked pasta to the slow cooker and stir everything together. 
    7. Taste and add more Parmesan Garlic sauce if needed. 
    8. Top with cooked chopped bacon.
