  ingredients_markdown: |-
    * 1 loaf french bread 
    * 1/4 cup mayo 
    * 2 cups shredded mozzarella 
    * 4-6 cloves garlic 
    * 1 stick softened butter
    * italian seasoning 
  directions_markdown: |-
    1. Preheat oven to 400F\
    2. Slice bread longways
    3. Combine mayo, half mozzarella, garlic, and butter
    4. Spread on each inside half of the bread 
    5. Sprinkle evenly with the remaining shredded mozzarella 
    6. Sprinkle with italian seasoning
    7. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until cheese is lightly browned and bubbly
