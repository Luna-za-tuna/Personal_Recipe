---
title: Homemade Beef Jerky
categories: 
  - Snacks
date: 2022-12-28
featured_image:
recipe:
  servings: 12-14
  prep: 12-14 minutes
  cook: 3 hours
  ingredients_markdown: |-
    * 3 lb eye of round roast
    * 1 cup dark brown sugar
    * 1 cup soy sauce
    * 3 tbsp worcestershire sauce
    * 1 tbsp smoked paprika 
    * 1 tsp unseasoned meat tenderizer
    * 1 tsp ground black pepper
    * 1 tsp red pepper flakes
    * 1 tsp onion powder
    * 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  directions_markdown: |-
    1. Slice meat between 1/8 and 1/4 inch thick with the grain (put in freezer before hand to make slicing easier)
    2. make the marinade by mixing everything together
    3. Add the meat to the marinade and toss all to coat evenly. marinate for at least 12 hours or overnight
    4. Preheat oven to 175F
    5. Line baking sheet and put a rack on top of the sheet
    6. Add the meat to the rack in a single layer
    7. Put the meat in oven and rotate throughout the next 3-4 hours
