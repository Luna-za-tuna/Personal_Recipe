  ingredients_markdown: |-
    * **Soups:**
    * 1 tbsp olive oil
    * 1 lb Italian sausage 
    * 1 yellow onion, diced
    * 6 cloves garlic, minced or finely grated
    * 5 tbsp tomato paste
    * 24 oz can crushed tomatoes
    * 8 tsp chicken bouillon
    * 1 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
    * 1/2 tsp garlic powder
    * 1/2 tsp onion powder
    * Salt and Pepper to taste
    * 8 cups water
    * 1 cup heavy cream
    * 1/2 lb Mafalda corta pasta (or lasagna noodles)
    * Fresh parsley, chopped 
    * **Ricotta Mix:**
    * 6 oz ricotta
    * 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella
    * 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
    * Italian seasoning
    * Salt and Pepper
  directions_markdown: |-
    1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. 
    2. Add the Italian sausage and lightly brown it. 
    3. Add diced onions and saut√© for a few minutes. 
    4. Next, add the minced garlic, cooking it for just a minute and being careful not to burn it. 
    5. Stir in the tomato paste and cook for another minute or two. 
    6. Add the bouillon and dry seasonings, mixing everything well. 
    7. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and water. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer and cook covered for 15 minutes. 
    8. Stir in the heavy cream until well combined. 
    9. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions. 
    10. Mix Ricotta ingredients.
    11. Just before serving, stir in the parsley. 
    12. Top the soup with the ricotta mixture and fresh Parmesan.
