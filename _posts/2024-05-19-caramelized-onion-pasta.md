recipe:
  servings: n/a
  prep: n/a
  cook: n/a
  ingredients_markdown: |-
    * 1 lb (500g) white onions thinly sliced (about 3 large onions)
    * 1/4 cup (60 ml) olive oil
    * 2 tbsp balsamic glaze
    * 1/4 tsp salt
    * 1/4 tsp pepper
    * 1 bulb garlic
    * 12 oz (350g) pasta
    * 4.4 oz (125g) sundried tomatoes(diced)
    * 1 small bunch of fresh basil (finely diced)
    * 5.3 oz (150g) mascarpone
    * 1/3 cup (30g) grated parmesan
  directions_markdown: |-
    1. Preheat oven to 350F
    2. In a large baking dish, add sliced onion, 1/4 cup olive oil, 2 tbsp balsamic glaze, salt, and pepper
    3. Mix well and spread into thin even layer across pan
    4. Cover with foil and place into the oven for about an hour and 10-15 minutes or until onions are soft, sticky, and sweet. Make sure to stir the mixture around periodically
    5. Slice the tip of the garlic off to expose cloves and cover with oil
    6. Wrap in foil and place in oven for about an hour (you can check them after about 45 minutes to make sure they dont burn)
    7. Cook pasta per instructions and save a cup of pasta water
    8. Once onions and garlic are done, squeeze garlic into baking dish with onions and mash the garlic up before mixing
    9. Mix in tomatoes and mascarpone and some pasta water
    10. Stir in cooked pasta until combined then lightly stir in parmesan and basil
    11. You can add more pasta water if needed to thin out to your liking
    12. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper to your liking
  notes_markdown: |-
    * If you notice the onions starting to burn before they are caramelizing you can turn the temperature down a bit. Its harder to go too low than too hot.
