  ingredients_markdown: |-
    * 1 sheet of puff pastry
    * 4 small redskin potatoes (diced)
    * 5 chicken thighs
    * 10 oz beef sausage
    * Cajun seasoning (ingredients on screen)
    * 1/4 cup flour
    * 3/4 cup vegetable oil
    * 1 tsp chicken base
    * 2 cups water
    * 1 tbsp tomato paste
    * 1 diced green bell pepper
    * 1/2 diced onion
    * 3 stalks celery (diced)
    * 3 cloves minced garlic
    * **Cajun butter:**
    * 3 tbsp melted unsalted butter
    * 1/2 tsp black pepper
    * 1/2 tsp garlic powder
    * 1/2 tsp onion powder
    * 1/2 tsp paprika
    * 2 tsp hot sauce (optional)
    * Fresh parsley 
  directions_markdown: |-
    1. Heat a pot over medium heat
    2. Slice sausage into thin rounds and cube chicken
    3. Add oil to pot and add sausage. Cook till browned 
    4. Once sausage is cooked, remove and add in chicken
    5. Once its mostly cooked, season with 1 tsp black pepper, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1 tsp paprika, 1 tbsp salt, 1/2 tsp thyme
    6. Cook for another minute or two and then remove and add 3/4 cup of oil to the pot and set to medium-low
    7. Add in 1/4 cup flour to the pot and stir in. Youll want to periodically stir this for 20-30 minutes until it turns a nice dark brown.
    8. While waiting, dice celery and onion. Once flour is done cooking down add in the celery and onion
    9. Cook till onions soften
    10. Drop in tomato paste, minced garlic, diced potatoes, water, and chicken base
    11. Mix then add back in the chicken and sausage. Season with 1 tsp black pepper, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder, 1 tsp paprika, 1/2 tbsp salt, 1/2 tsp thyme
    12. Mix and cook for approx 20 minutes, stirring every 5 minutes
    13. Preheat oven to 425F
    14. Place into baking dish and cover with puff pastry. Slice vents diagonally 2 inches apart the length of the baking dish
    15. Mix ingredients for the cajun butter and then brush on the puff pastry
    16. Place in oven for 20-25 minutes
