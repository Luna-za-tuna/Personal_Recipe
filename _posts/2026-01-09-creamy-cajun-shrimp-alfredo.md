  ingredients_markdown: |-
    * 1 lb jumbo shrimp
    * 1 lb cooked pasta
    * 1 1/2 cup heavy cream
    * 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
    * 1/2 cup diced bell peppers
    * 1 tbsp garlic paste
    * 1-2 tsp cayenne pepper
    * 1-2 tsp black pepper
    * 1-2 tsp salt
    * 1-2 tsp ground cumin
    * 1-2 tsp garlic powder 
  directions_markdown: |-
    1. Cook pasta to desired doneness
    2. Put shrimp in a bowl and season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, cumin, and cayenne
    3. Heat pan with oil and once hot, add shrimp and cook for a couple minutes on each side
    4. Remove shrimp and add in diced bell pepper
    5. Once the peppers have some color, add in garlic and butter
    6. Add in all the same seasonings as the shrimp
    7. Mix and add in heavy cream
    8. Once simmering add in parmesan cheese
    9. once melted, add back in pasta and shrimp and serve
